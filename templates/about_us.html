About Us

Introduction

Welcome to VisBrain! We are a dedicated team of researchers and developers working together to leverage artificial intelligence for medical advancements. Our project focuses on brain tumor classification using cutting-edge deep learning techniques.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate and reliable tools for medical professionals to assist in the early detection and classification of brain tumors. We aim to improve patient outcomes by integrating AI technology into the medical field.

Our Journey

The VisBrain project began in 2023 as a collaborative effort between the Faculty of Computer and Artificial Intelligence at Cairo University and various medical institutions. Over time, our project has evolved, integrating more advanced technologies and expanding our dataset to improve model accuracy.

Technologies We Use

Our project utilizes a variety of technologies, including:

  • 1- Python
  • 2- TensorFlow and Keras for deep learning
  • 3- Flask for the web application
  • 4- HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the front-end
